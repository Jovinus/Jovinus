### About

I am a PhD candidate in **Digital Health** at [SAIHST, Sungkyunkwan University](https://saihst.skku.edu/), advised by **Prof. Byung-Jae Lee**, and an **AI Researcher at [AITRICS](https://www.aitrics.com)**. In 2025 I co-founded [**BreathYou**](https://breathyou.care), a respiratory digital health startup translating these research directions toward clinical practice.

My work centers on turning messy, longitudinal clinical data into **deployable** decision-support systems — models that survive regulatory review and behave well at the bedside, not only on a held-out test set.

### Research Interests

- **Clinical time-series deep learning** — Transformers for longitudinal pulmonary function trajectories, early-warning prediction (sepsis, cardiac arrest, AKI), and robust label engineering
- **Medical AI regulation (SaMD)** — lessons from two MFDS-approved devices and one FDA-cleared system; prospective external validation and real-world monitoring
- **Respiratory digital health** — Korean-specific reference equations, a multimodal respiratory foundation model, and CDSS for pulmonary function decline
- **Signal-level physiology** — HRV / ECG, EMG, and multimodal affective computing

### Currently Working On

- 🫁 **DeepBreath CDSS** — predicting pulmonary function decline trajectories from longitudinal PFT data
- 📝 **MediPipe** — OCR / NLP pipeline for medical record structuring
- 🩺 Transformer-based cardiac arrest & sepsis prediction for general wards, with a focus on prospective validation
